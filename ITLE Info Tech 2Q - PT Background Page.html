Background Study

The project that we proposed is something that will help the Madrasah and the community near it. It has come to our attention that every time it floods they can't have a faster way to drain or throw the water out. So we proposed a system that can help the Madrasah community drain or reroute the water so the flooding will be less-effective than last time.
