Our Concerns

One of Madrasah's concerns is the common flooding in their schools, since they are stationed near the Davao River.
The aftermath leaves disarray in the campus, leaving muddy paths which makes it hard for people to travel in, and overall a hindrance in the school environment.

Our Project

Our project, Madrasah Rush, aims to utilize the energy free pumps and repair pipes
to make the area less flooded, since the Davao river bank recently built its walls higher.
Since then, the pumps will help the community of Madrasah to clear out any remaining water
from the area to provide a cleaner and efficient environment for learning students and working staff.
